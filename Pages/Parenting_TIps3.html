Create A Schedule For Them

  • Creating A schedule for your children. Having that kind of daily routine can reduce their stress and anxiety. They know what to do instead of running and playing around the house like a monkey and you don't need to say what they have to do next from behind like your hair is on fire. They're motivated to do their tasks on their hand and their confidence and independence can also be improved.

Using Timer

  • Using Timer for your children is a good habit. They will able to do their tasks on time and focus on their work. They will become more organized like they won't be wasting their time when they do something. They won't have stress and see their main destination and achieved things with less mistakes.

Introducing Planner Or Schedule Tools

  • Introducing Planner Or Schedule Tools to children is a good thing. It's not too early to learn for children. They will know how to manage time usefully in a very young age and understand the value of time. It's essential for kids because it enhances organization, independence, and responsibility. keep them focused and productive. It can improve your planning skills also. Finish their tasks in time and know what to do next.

Limiting Their Screen Time

  • Limiting Their Screen Time is really important things that every parents should do. You can encourage your children to explore the world and build meaningful connections with others. It also allows families to spend more time together. Children will be able to control themselves and improve discipline. They can do physical activities that is good for their health and brain.

Giving Them Time-limitation Tasks

  • Compeleting their time-limitation tasks helps children to improve their time management, focus, and discipline. It teaches them to prioritize tasks, make quick decisions, and stay organized. It can boost their confidence, and learn to handle pressure by working within a set time. These skills prepare them to meet deadlines and tackle future challenges.

Break Tasks Into Small Pieces

  • Breaking tasks into small pieces helps children stay focused and not feel overwhelmed. It makes tasks easier to manage and boosts their confidence as they complete each step. This also helps them build better planning and time management skills.

Teach Time-Blocking Techniques

  • Teaching time-blocking techniquesis one of the essential lessons for their future. Time blocking method is a time management technique that involves dividing your day into blocks of time, each dedicated to a specific task or group of tasks. This method can help you stay focused, get things done, and prioritize your tasks.


    Time blocking is a time management method where you divide your day into blocks of time. Each block is dedicated to a specific task or group of tasks. For example, you might block out 9 am to 10 am for checking and responding to emails, 10 am to 12 pm for working on a specific project, and 1 pm to 2 pm to have lunch at that new Thai restaurant.

Let Them Have Long-term Plans And Goals

  • Letingt them have long-term plans and goals gives them clear direction and purpose. It will help them to stay focused, manage their time better, and track their progress. Goals keep them motivated and provide clarity in decision-making. With a clear plan, they can stay on track and work steadily toward what they want to achieve.

Make Time For Them

  • Making Time for them is also important thing. Recently, because parents are becoming busier, there are times when they are unable to give attention to their children. They often get so absorbed in their work, using their valuable time to earn money, that they cannot spend time with their children. Children enjoy spending time with their parents, playing and going places together. Although children want to live happily with their parents, they often suppress their desires when they observe the pressures and struggles of their parents. At first, this may not be an issue, but as time passes, the lack of connection with their parents can lead to feelings of isolation and affect their mental health. Therefore, parents should spend time with their children and create happy memories.

Show that your Love is Unconditional

  • Showing that your Love is Unconditional is also essential things. As a parent, it is your responsibility to guide your children in the right direction. Raise your children to be people who have great love for each other. If you want your children to grow up to be like this man, you must give them kindness and love. When you have a problem, you should not take the blame.
